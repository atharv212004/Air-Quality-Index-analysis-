The Air Quality Index (AQI) Analysis Project focuses on analyzing environmental pollution data to understand air quality patterns, pollutant concentration trends, and environmental health indicators.

Air pollution is a major environmental issue affecting human health and climate. By analyzing AQI data, this project aims to identify pollution trends, understand pollutant impact, and generate insights that can help in environmental monitoring and policy planning.
The analysis explores key pollutants such as:

PM2.5

PM10

NO₂

SO₂

CO

O₃

These pollutants are analyzed across different locations and time periods to study variations in air quality.
